Tom Yum Soup

Preparation Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients



















Instructions

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Pour the stock into a deep cooking pot over medium-high heat. Add the prepared lemongrass, including the upper sections of the stalk you didn't mince, boil for 5 to 6 minutes to soften.
  3. Add carrot, garlic, onion, galangal or ginger, lime leaves, 1 1/2 tablespoons of fish sauce, soy sauce, and lime juice. Reduce heat slightly, cover with lid and continue to let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Add mushrooms and zucchini, and let it simmer for another 5 minutes.
  5. Add shrimp and cherry tomatoes. Cook 3 minutes or until shrimp are pink and plump.
  6. Turn down the heat to low and add 1/4 can coconut milk, chilli oil (optional), brown sugar (optional), red pepper flakes (optional).
  7. Add the remaining teaspoon of fish sauce. Discard the lime leaves. Serve with fresh silantro.
