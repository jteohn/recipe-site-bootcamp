Tofu Stir-fry

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients










Instructions

  1. Drain the tofu. Wrap each block in a double layer of paper towels and pat dry, pressing down on the tofu lightly to squeeze out excess moisture. Cut the tofu into 3/4-inch cubes.
  2. In a large non-stick pan, heat the canola oil over medium-high heat. Once the oul is hot but not smoking, add the tofu (be careful here as the oil will splatter a little) and drizzle with 1 tablespoon soy sauce. Saut√©, stirring every minute or so until the tofu is nicely colored on all sides and the moisture has cooked off (~8-10 minutes).
  3. Add garlic, roughly 2/3 of green onion, ginger, chilli paste, and the remaining 2 tablespoons soy sauce. Stir and cook until fragrant (~1 minute).
  4. Add spinach, stirring as you go so that it wilts and you can fit more in the pan. Once the first batch has wilted, continute to add and wilt the spinach until all the spinach is added. Stir in sesame seeds and sesame oil.
  5. Remove from heat. Sprinkle the reserved green onions over the top. Serve and enjoy!
